Engineering Novel Sensors for a Healthier & Safer World

Welcome to the Advanced Electronics and Sensor Devices Lab. We operate at the intersection of physics, chemistry, and electronics to create cutting-edge biosensors and nanomaterial-based devices for critical applications in healthcare and food safety.

Aaryashree (Aarya)
