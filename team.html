AESD Lab is led by Dr. Aaryashree and welcomes undergraduate, master’s, and PhD students interested in sensors, flexible electronics, and functional nanomaterials.

Principal Investigator

Dr. Aaryashree

Associate Professor — Advanced Electronics & Sensor Devices Lab, College of Engineering, Shibaura Institute of Technology

Dr. Aaryashree leads the Advanced Electronics & Sensor Devices (AESD) Lab, where research spans flexible and printed electronic devices, electrochemical and optical sensors, and functional nanomaterials for gas sensing, optoelectronics, and photovoltaics.

Opportunities

Join the lab

AESD Lab welcomes motivated students for bachelor’s, master’s, and PhD research in sensors, flexible electronics, and functional nanomaterials. Students in the Innovative Global Program and other departments are encouraged to get in touch with a brief introduction and statement of research interests. Joint supervision and collaborative projects with other laboratories are welcome where topics overlap.

For inquiries, write to aarya@shibaura-it.ac.jp.
