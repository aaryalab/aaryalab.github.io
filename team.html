Principal Investigator

Dr. Aaryashree

Associate Professor — Advanced Electronics & Sensor Devices Lab, College of Systems Engineering and Science, Shibaura Institute of Technology

Dr. Aaryashree leads the Advanced Electronics & Sensor Devices (AESD) Lab, where research spans flexible and printed electronic devices, electrochemical and optical sensors, and functional nanomaterials for gas sensing, optoelectronics, and photovoltaics.

She completed a BTech in Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering at Annamalai University (2008–2012) and a PhD in Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Indore (2014–2019), working on functionalized nanomaterials for gas and optoelectronic sensors. A JSPS Postdoctoral Fellowship (2020) brought her to the Department of Applied Chemistry at Shibaura Institute of Technology, where she developed molecularly-imprinted, disposable electrochemical sensors for therapeutic drug and food safety monitoring — work that has since been covered by international science press.

She continues this work as Associate Professor at Shibaura Institute of Technology, supervising student research and teaching across physics, biophysics, and biomedical engineering for the College of Systems Engineering and Science and the Innovative Global Program.

Opportunities

Join the lab

AESD Lab welcomes motivated students for bachelor’s, master’s, and PhD research in sensors, flexible electronics, and functional nanomaterials. Students in the Innovative Global Program and other departments are encouraged to get in touch with a brief introduction and statement of research interests. Joint supervision and collaborative projects with other laboratories are welcome where topics overlap.
