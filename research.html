Integration & Approach

Materials → Sensors → Intelligence

Each project pipeline progresses from nanomaterial synthesis through sensor prototyping to ML-enhanced signal processing—creating an integrated ecosystem rather than isolated experiments.

  • 1. Printed Nanomaterials: MXene inks, graphene composites, ZnO nanostructures
  • 2. Device Fabrication: Electrochemical, flexible, wire-type, paper-based architectures
  • 3. Signal Intelligence: Machine learning, neural networks, edge computing

Cross-Project Synergies

  • TDM → Food Safety

    MIP sensor platform adapted from antibacterial agents to food freshness indicators

  • Kakenhi → NARO

    Wire-type sensor design builds on paper-based prototypes from SIT funding

  • Eisai Validation

    Clinical accuracy protocols inform all pharmaceutical and food-grade applications
